Cross-Asset Momentum Strategy

Spring 2026
In Progress

Rank-weighted momentum strategy across 14 ETFs spanning commodities, equity sectors, fixed income, and currencies. Uses trailing 12-month returns with an EMA trend confirmation filter. Three-tier signal weighting (STRONG/NORMAL/WEAKENING as 1.3x/1.0x/0.5x rank multipliers), 25% single-asset concentration cap, 2% turnover buffer, and a graduated macro regime filter that scales exposure based on safe-haven asset signals.

Backtested over 14 years with a Sharpe ratio of 0.78 and +80% cumulative momentum spread. Currently running a 4-week live test on Interactive Brokers with $50M simulated capital for a graduate-level quantitative investment course at Duke. Iterated through eight design versions after critique that fixed weekly rebalancing was incompatible with momentum dynamics.

Python (pandas, NumPy, yfinance), Interactive Brokers TWS/API

Econometric Modelling & Risk Analysis

Fall 2025
Completed

Analyzed JPMorgan Chase equity returns against the DJIA index (June 2017 - June 2023). Estimated market beta (~1.2) through linear regression with Breusch-Pagan and Jarque-Bera diagnostics for heteroscedasticity and normality, comparing full-period vs pre-COVID results to identify structural shifts.

Selected ARMA(4,5) via AIC grid search with Ljung-Box residual validation. GARCH(1,1) via BIC search for volatility clustering. Extended into a VAR(9) framework across JPM, Goldman Sachs, American Express, and Visa to analyze cross-asset shock transmission via impulse response and Granger causality.

Python (statsmodels, pandas, NumPy)

About
