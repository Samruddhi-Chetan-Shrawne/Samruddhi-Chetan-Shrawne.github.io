In Progress

Risk Anchor

A portfolio rebalancing tool that monitors drift against target allocations and triggers rules-based rebalancing across equity, fixed income, and alternative asset classes. Designed around a transparent risk scoring engine with full allocation explainability, challenging the opacity of existing robo-advisory platforms. The thesis: retail investors deserve the same visibility into allocation logic that institutional desks take for granted.

In Progress

Cross-Commodity Momentum & Term Structure Strategy

Developing a double-sort strategy that combines price momentum with futures term structure signals across commodities and macro-ETFs: gold, crude oil, copper, and natural gas. Backtesting long-short and long-only variants with momentum-weighted allocation on Interactive Brokers paper trading ($50M simulated). Investigating whether term structure signals improve Sharpe ratios beyond standalone momentum in commodity cross-sections.

Factor Research Futures Backtesting Interactive Brokers Python

Completed

Econometric Modelling & Risk Analysis

Modeled volatility dynamics on JPMorgan Chase equity returns using ARMA/GARCH frameworks, with stationarity diagnostics (ADF, KPSS), volatility clustering analysis, and out-of-sample return forecasting. Extended the work into a multivariate setting with a Vector Autoregression model to estimate cross-asset transmission effects, performing impulse response and variance decomposition analysis for portfolio risk attribution.

About Me

I'm a M.Eng. Financial Technology candidate at Duke's Pratt School of Engineering with a background spanning WealthTech, capital markets, and regulatory technology in India.

My work sits at the intersection of quantitative research and product thinking. I care about understanding the structure of markets, not just building tools on top of them. Whether it's modeling volatility dynamics, researching momentum signals in commodity futures, or designing transparent allocation engines, the through-line is the same: making complex financial systems more legible.

At Duke, I hold a Graduate Merit Scholarship, serve as a TA for capstone courses, and am a member of the Pratt Peers Advisory Board.
