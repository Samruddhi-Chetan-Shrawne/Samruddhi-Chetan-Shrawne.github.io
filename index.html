Risk Anchor

A portfolio rebalancing tool for self-directed investors. Monitors your portfolio against target allocations, calculates drift and risk metrics (beta, volatility, Sharpe ratio), and generates rebalancing recommendations when you exceed your threshold. Built because existing solutions either lock you into a robo-advisor or force you to track everything manually in spreadsheets. This gives you the transparency and control without the advisory fees. In Progress

Cross-Asset Momentum Strategy

Building a quantitative momentum strategy that ranks and allocates across 14 ETFs spanning commodities, equity sectors, fixed income, and currencies. The model uses trailing 12-month returns to identify persistent winners and an exponential moving average filter to confirm trend strength before overweighting positions. Designed as long-only with a macro regime overlay that scales exposure based on safe-haven asset signals. Backtested over 14 years with a Sharpe ratio of 0.77 and significantly lower drawdowns than traditional benchmarks. Currently running a 4-week live test on Interactive Brokers with $50M simulated capital for a graduate-level quantitative investment course at Duke.

Econometric Modelling & Risk Analysis

Analyzed JPMorgan Chase equity returns against the DJIA index (June 2017 - June 2023). Estimated market beta through linear regression with heteroscedasticity and normality diagnostics, comparing full-period vs pre-COVID results to identify structural shifts. Modeled return dynamics with an AIC-optimized ARMA(4,5) and conditional volatility with GARCH(1,1), selected through a BIC search across specifications. Extended into a VAR(9) framework across JPM, Goldman Sachs, American Express, and Visa to analyze cross-asset shock transmission via impulse response and Granger causality analysis.
